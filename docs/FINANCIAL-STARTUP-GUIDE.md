## üè¶ Funding Options

### 1. Self-Funded (Recommended Start)
**Pros:** No debt, full ownership, fast decisions
**Cons:** Limited by personal capital

**Strategy:**
- Start with 1-2 machines from savings
- Reinvest profits to fund machines 3-5
- Timeline: 5 machines in 12-18 months

### 2. 0% Intro APR Credit Cards
**Best cards for business equipment:**
- Chase Ink Business Unlimited (0% for 12 mo)
- Amex Blue Business Plus (0% for 12 mo)
- Capital One Spark (0% for 12 mo)

**Strategy:**
- Open 2-3 cards, get $15-25K total credit
- Buy machines on 0% cards
- Pay off before interest kicks in
- **Risk:** Must have income to make payments if business slow

### 3. Equipment Financing
**Ask VapeTM directly** - many equipment vendors offer:
- 12-36 month financing
- 10-20% down, rest financed
- Rates: 8-15% APR

**Other options:**
- Balboa Capital
- Currency Capital
- Clicklease

### 4. Personal Loan
- LightStream: 6-8% APR, no fees
- SoFi: 7-12% APR
- Marcus by Goldman Sachs: 7-15% APR

**Borrow:** $20-30K for 3-5 machines
**Payment:** ~$400-600/month
**Covered by:** 2-3 profitable machines

### 5. SBA Microloan
- Up to $50,000
- 6-9% interest
- Requires business plan
- Takes 2-4 weeks

### 6. Friends & Family
**Structure it properly:**
- Formal loan agreement with interest
- OR equity stake (e.g., 20% for $20K)
- Clear terms on paper
